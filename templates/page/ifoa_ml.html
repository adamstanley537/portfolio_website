Volunteering
for the IFoA

This page details the article I wrote when volunteering for the IFoA' Machine Learning in Reserving working party.


While working as an actuary I volunteered to work for the IFoA's Machine Learning in reserving working party. Admittedly I did so wit the hope it would further skills and knowledge alongside helping out where I can. I realised that, when looking at it through this lens I could use the resources that the working party had put out to learn the machine learning techniques I was supposed to be help investigate and at the same time write a blog detailing my process and helping them through the journey I took and avoid main of the pitfalls. So that's what I did. The blog was officially published by the working party on the 31st of October.

My Machine Learning in Reserving Journey

I hope to go back to the working party at some point, and help out again.
