Here's a little on what I've been up to for the last few years


Hi there, I am Adam. Welcome to my website! I am a data scientist living in Cambodia with my partner, where we recently relocated after taking some time off to travel. I studied Maths at the University of Bristol and then spent 4 years working as an Actuary in London, where I qualified as a fellow of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries. In my later years working, I started switching my focus from actuarial specific data science to more general data science, as I volunteered for the Machine Learning in Reserving working party (see my page on the under the actuarial tab).

In the meantime, I concentrated on my coding skills in Python and SQL to enhance my use of data analytics and machine learning models. I have been steadily building my portfolio, all of which is displayed on this website. I am focussing on my interests, namely machine learning and data viz, and if possible doing so with an environmental twist.

Outside of work, I am passionate about the outdoors. My favourite activities include trail running/hiking, skiing, diving and anything that involves the sea. While in Cambodia I am hoping to run a 100km ultra marathon around the temples of Angkor Wat, learn to kitesurf in Kampot and to continue exploring all that South East Asia has to offer.
